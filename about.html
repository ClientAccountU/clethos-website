About

Built for how business moves money

We’re building treasury and payment infrastructure so businesses can collect, hold, and pay globally—faster, cheaper, and with full visibility.

Mission

Cross-border payments are slow, costly, and opaque. We give businesses infrastructure that settles in minutes, at a fraction of traditional cost, with full visibility—so you can focus on growth, not wiring.

Idea to Market

Building treasury infrastructure so businesses can collect, hold, and pay globally—faster, cheaper, and with full visibility.

December '24

We started with a simple idea: cross-border payments are slow, costly, and opaque. Businesses deserve infrastructure that settles in minutes, at a fraction of traditional cost—multi-currency accounts, customizable wallets, and stablecoin rails without the complexity, and with the best security systems available.

January '25

Our founding team came together and we secured the regulatory foundation to offer compliant treasury and payment infrastructure—so we could serve businesses with full visibility and bank-grade security and compliance.

April '25

We closed our pre-seed round and ran our first live cross-border flows. Real settlements in minutes: the kind of secure, scalable money movement we’d set out to build for collections, payouts, and global treasury.

July '25

We welcomed strategic advisors and went to market with one promise: treasury infrastructure for modern business. From collections and payouts to customizable wallets and multi-currency accounts—one stack, dashboard, API or appointed consultant, built for how business moves money.
