About CLETHOS

Built for how business moves money today

Cross-border payments are slow, costly, and opaque. We give businesses treasury and payment infrastructure that settles in minutes, at a fraction of traditional cost, with full visibility.

Our platform combines multi-currency fiat accounts, customizable wallets, and stablecoin rails (including USDT, USDC and other leading assets) so you can collect, hold, and pay globally from a single stack—maximising cross-border liquidity and financial fluidity, via dashboard, API or appointed consultant.

Expertise

Finance is shifting on-chain

The cross-border FX market is being rebuilt on-chain. Stablecoins, tokenized deposits, and programmable money are unlocking real-time settlement, lower fees, and richer data—without asking businesses to become blockchain experts.

We focus on B2B use cases where on-chain rails make a tangible difference: high-volume collections and payouts, corporate treasury, and programmable flows like splits, escrow, and revenue sharing. CLETHOS helps you operate borderlessly and internationally—lifting traditional banking restrictions without sacrificing bank-grade security and compliance. We abstract the wallets, keys, and chains so your teams work with familiar currencies and dashboards.
