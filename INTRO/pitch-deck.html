Who we are

CLETHOS at a glance

CLETHOS is treasury infrastructure for modern business—helping B2B and B2C operators move, store, and spend money across borders with speed, visibility, and bank-grade security.

The full platform

  • Cross-border payments — collections and payouts that settle in minutes, not days, on compliant rails
  • Multi-currency accounts & wallets — dedicated fiat balances and customizable wallets in one stack
  • Stablecoin infrastructure — USDT, USDC, and other leading assets alongside fiat for liquidity and programmable flows
  • Treasury & FX — hold, convert, and optimise balances; partner-enabled access to global currency and settlement networks
  • Programmable money — splits, escrow, revenue sharing, and API/dashboard control without managing keys and chains yourself

Why this deck focuses on Introductions

Not every business needs the full CLETHOS rollout on day one. CLETHOS Introductions is our alternative path: we connect you with closely vetted, in-house partners for FX, banking, treasury—or a single unified partner who covers the full fiat stack—so you work directly with the right provider while we handle matching and a fair, escrow-backed onboarding fee.

The opportunity

You don’t always need the full stack

Many businesses need a single capability executed well—FX, banking access, treasury liquidity—not an end-to-end platform on day one.

  • Full onboarding is heavy when the immediate need is narrow (one corridor, one product)
  • Finding a regulated partner in a new market takes months of research and compliance
  • Open directories and cold outreach rarely offer vetted, in-house relationships
  • Wrong partner fit wastes time, fees, and internal legal review
  • Teams still need expert FX, banking, or liquidity—without rebuilding treasury from zero

Introductions is the lighter path: one curated partner, one escrow-backed fee, direct commercial terms.
